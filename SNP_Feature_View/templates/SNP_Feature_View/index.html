AG at this position in the genome, indicates a flush response to alcohol.

Alcohol flush is often colloquially referred to as 'Asian Glow.'

AG, as above, indicates this individual will experience alcohol flush.

AA, would also indicate alcohol flush reaction.

GG, no flush reaction.
